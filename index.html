Privacy Policy — PR Journey

Effective date: 11 June 2026
App: PR Journey (package com.ynnossence.appsrc)
Contact: ynnossence@gmail.com

PR Journey (“the app”, “we”) helps you track your Australian permanent‑residency journey. This policy explains what data the app handles. In short: the app is local‑first — your information is stored on your device, and we do not operate any server that collects or stores it.

1. Information stored on your device

The app stores the following locally on your device only (in an on‑device database and file storage). It is not transmitted to us:

  • Profile data you enter: name, age, occupation, English level, education, work experience, partner status, current visa and expiry, nominating state.
  • Documents you track and any files/photos you attach to them.
  • EOIs, milestones and timeline entries you create.
  • AI assistant chat history.
  • App settings, including an optional AI API key (see §3).

We do not have an account system and we do not receive a copy of this data. Uninstalling the app, or using the in‑app clear/delete actions, removes it.

2. Information collected by third parties

The app uses the following third‑party services. Their data handling is governed by their own policies:

a) Advertising — Google AdMob (free tier only)

The free version shows a single banner ad via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device and advertising identifiers and ad‑interaction data to serve and measure ads. The app requests non‑personalised ads by default. Buying the one‑time “remove ads” option disables ads entirely.
Google’s policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

b) Purchases — RevenueCat & the app stores

The optional “remove ads” purchase is processed by Google Play Billing / Apple App Store and managed through RevenueCat, which records a purchase/entitlement receipt (and an anonymous purchase identifier) so your purchase can be restored. We do not receive your payment card details.
RevenueCat: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

c) AI assistant — Anthropic (only if you opt in)

By default the AI assistant answers offline using on‑device logic — nothing leaves your device. If you choose to add your own Claude (Anthropic) API key, then when you send a message the app sends that message together with the relevant profile context to Anthropic to generate a reply. Your API key is stored in your device’s secure storage and is used only for this purpose.
Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy

3. Device permissions

  • Camera / Photos — only used so you can attach documents or photos to your checklist. Attached files are stored on your device; we do not upload them.

4. Children

PR Journey is intended for adults managing a migration process. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Security

Your data lives on your device. The optional AI API key is held in the platform’s encrypted secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain).

6. Your choices and rights

Because your data is stored locally, you remain in control of it:

  • Edit or delete entries at any time within the app.
  • Remove the AI key in Profile to stop any data being sent to Anthropic.
  • Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data.
  • Opt out of personalised ads through your device’s ad‑settings, or remove ads via the in‑app purchase.

Depending on your region (e.g. EU/UK GDPR, California CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal data. Since we do not hold your data on a server, you can exercise these directly on your device; for questions, contact us below.

7. Disclaimer

PR Journey is an independent tool and is not affiliated with the Australian Government or the Department of Home Affairs. Points and invitation‑round figures are estimates to help you plan and are not immigration advice.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the effective date above and within the app listing.
